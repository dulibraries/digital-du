How to contribute to Digital CC

If you have materials that that should be preserved as part of Colorado College's history, please contact Sarah Bogard, Digital Innovations Librarian, at sarah.bogard@coloradocollege.edu or call 719-389-6875 to make arrangements for file transfer.

It is impossible to collect everything digital produced on campus! However, official publications of the college as well as scholarship of students and faculty are given top priority. We collect the following and more:

Student publications (Catalyst, Cipher, Leviathan, Alpine Journal and more)

Academic departments and programs (newsletters, department histories)

Campus events (First Mondays, State of the Rockies, Big Idea)

Student organizations on campus

Student theses and capstones (including student films)

Athletic team and player photographs

Statistics about the College

Commencement and convocation videos

Podcasts and videos
